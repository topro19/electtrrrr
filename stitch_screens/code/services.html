Renovation & New Construction

Whether it concerns a minor adjustment or a completely new installation, at JDV Elektrotechniek we are happy to work with you to realize your wishes.

Telecom: Data network, Intercom, Camera surveillance

JDV Elektrotechniek has Comelit as its preferred brand for modifying or upgrading door intercom and camera surveillance systems. Thanks to our experience with Comelit, we can deliver a high-quality system that is completely up-to-date.

Sustainability: Charging stations

Need a charging station? JDV Elektrotechniek is a certified Wallbox installer. We work with the best products from Wallbox. Thanks to our experience and good relationship with Wallbox, we offer a fast and high-quality solution for a reliable charging system.

Distribution boards: Replacement & Expansion

The distribution board is a crucial component of your installation. An outdated distribution board usually no longer meets current safety standards. We replace or modify your distribution board so that you can rest assured of a properly functioning and safe installation.
